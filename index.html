About Us

The Computer Society of India (CSI) was formed in 1965 and has been dedicated to promoting the education and exchange of information within the IT industry. Our teams work together to bring innovation, collaboration, and quality in everything we do. Below are the key teams driving our organization forward.

Our Teams

Manages all media communications and social media channels.

Handles all membership registrations and renewals.

Design Team

Responsible for creating all visuals and promotional material.

Event Team

Organizes workshops, seminars, and major events.

Content Team

Develops content for blogs, newsletters, and publications.

Web Team

Maintains and develops the website.
